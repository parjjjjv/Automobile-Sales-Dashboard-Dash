# 💡 Key Insights – Automobile Sales Analysis

## 📉 Economic Impact

- Automobile sales peaked around 1996, likely driven by strong economic growth and favorable conditions such as lower oil prices

- Average sales values are consistently higher during non-recession periods, highlighting the impact of economic stability on consumer purchasing power

- A clear business cycle pattern is observed, with sales aligning closely with expansion and contraction phases of the economy

---

## 📊 Marketing & Demand Behavior

- Advertisement expenditure did not decline significantly during the 2008–09 recession, suggesting sustained marketing efforts despite reduced demand

---

## 🎯 Seasonal Trends

- Sales consistently peak during December, indicating strong seasonal influence driven by holiday demand

---

## 🧠 Key Takeaway

- Automobile sales are influenced by a combination of economic conditions, seasonal demand, and strategic marketing decisions, making context-driven analysis essential for accurate interpretation
