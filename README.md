# 🚗 Automobile Sales Analysis Dashboard (Recession vs Growth)

## 📊 Overview
This project presents an interactive dashboard to analyze automobile sales trends under different economic conditions, specifically comparing recession and non-recession periods.

The goal is to understand how external factors such as unemployment, advertising expenditure, and seasonal demand influence automobile sales.

---

## 🔍 Features
- 📈 Yearly and recession-based analysis
- 🎯 Dynamic filtering (year & report type)
- 📊 Multiple visualizations:
  - Sales trends over time
  - Vehicle-type performance
  - Advertising expenditure distribution
  - Unemployment vs sales relationship

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Automobile sales peaked around 1996, likely driven by strong economic growth and favorable market conditions  
- Advertisement expenditure remained relatively stable during the 2008–09 recession, indicating sustained marketing efforts  
- Average sales values are consistently higher during non-recession periods  
- Sales patterns align with economic business cycles (expansion and contraction phases)  
- Sales tend to peak in December, highlighting strong seasonal demand
